Our Story

The KalaLuxe Journey

Founded in 2025 in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, by designer Varsha Mittal, KalaLuxe was born from a passion for blending traditional hand-painting techniques with modern fashion silhouettes.

Our vision is to make artistic, hand-crafted fashion accessible to everyone without compromising on quality or aesthetics. We believe that luxury should be affordable, and that every woman deserves to wear a piece of art.

Each KalaLuxe creation is meticulously hand-painted by skilled artisans, making every piece unique and special - just like the woman who wears it.

Our Core Values

Artistry for All

We believe in making premium hand-painted designs accessible at mid-range prices, so more women can experience the joy of wearable art.

Modern Tradition

We honor our heritage by combining traditional techniques with contemporary designs, creating pieces that are both timeless and current.

Wearable Art

We see every fabric as a canvas, transforming ordinary garments into expressions of beauty and individuality.

Meet Our Founder

Varsha Mittal

A designer with a passion for traditional arts, Varsha founded KalaLuxe with the vision of making artistic fashion accessible to all women.

With a background in textile design and a love for hand-painting, she brings together the best of both worlds - traditional craftsmanship and contemporary aesthetics.

"I believe that every woman deserves to wear a piece of art that tells her unique story. At KalaLuxe, we're creating more than just clothes; we're creating experiences and memories."
