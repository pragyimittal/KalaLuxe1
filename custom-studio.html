Custom Studio

Design your own personalized hand-painted creations

Your Vision, Our Artistry

At KalaLuxe, we believe that personal style should be just that - personal. Our Custom Studio allows you to co-create with our artists to design a piece that perfectly reflects your unique taste and story.

Whether you want to add your initials to a saree, adjust the color palette to match a special occasion, or create a completely custom design, our team is here to bring your vision to life.

Customization costs start at just ₹500 and vary based on complexity and specific requirements.

The Customization Process

1

Select Your Base

Choose your product type (saree, clutch, or footwear) and the base material.

  • Sarees: Pure Georgette or Sheer Organza
  • Clutches: Various shapes and sizes available
  • Footwear: Juttis, block heels, or flats
2

Choose Your Motif

Select from our design library or suggest your own concept:

  • Floral patterns: roses, lotus, marigold
  • Abstract designs: geometric, contemporary
  • Traditional motifs: peacock, elephant, paisley
3

Add Personalization

Make it truly yours with these options:

  • Monograms or initials
  • Custom color palette
  • Special date or meaningful symbols
  • Blouse design adjustments (for sarees)
4

Review & Approve

Our design team will create a digital mockup for your approval before beginning the hand-painting process.
