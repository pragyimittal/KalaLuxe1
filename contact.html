Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to create a custom hand-painted saree?

Our custom hand-painted sarees typically take 2-3 weeks to complete, depending on the complexity of the design and our current order volume.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.

How do I care for my hand-painted products?

We recommend dry cleaning for all hand-painted items to preserve the artwork. Detailed care instructions are included with each purchase.

Can I visit your studio to see the products in person?

Yes, studio visits are available by appointment only. Please contact us to schedule a visit.
